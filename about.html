About Us

Kirana friends assist the Kirana owners in identifying their problems existing in their store and rectifying them with their help. In this journey, we help our customers build their future by making them ready for the changing market. Kirana owners, who have managed to transform or improve their stores to become modern neighborhood convenience stores, have not only managed to increase their business and profits but also impacted the lives of different stakeholders in the ecosystem.
